Our Mission

Our software team plans to engineer a chrome extension that automatically tracks the songs you listen to while you are working online. When it comes to a time where you must retain this information you can acsess a playlist of these songs to lisen to. You may ask, what is the point of this?

By connecting music to memories this will create a Proustian phenomenon. In other words through the sensory sense of sound ,in this case music, ot wo;; resurface the knowledge associated with it similar to how certain songs remind you of certain people or events. These music-memory connections are the foundation of our software.
